Frequently Asked Questions

What security services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive security services including manned guarding, patrol services, security systems installation, risk assessments, and emergency response. All services are tailored for mining, logistics, retail, and residential sectors in Northwest Tanzania.

How quickly can you deploy security personnel?

We can deploy security personnel within 24-48 hours for standard requests. Emergency security deployment is available 24/7 for urgent situations. Response times vary by location within Mwanza and Northwest Tanzania.

Are your security personnel trained and certified?

Yes, all our security personnel are professionally trained, licensed, and undergo regular training in security protocols, emergency response, and customer service. We maintain high standards in compliance with Tanzanian security regulations.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Mwanza and Northwest Tanzania, including Ilemela, Nyamagana, and surrounding regions. Our services extend to mining sites, lake ports, industrial facilities, and residential properties around Lake Victoria.

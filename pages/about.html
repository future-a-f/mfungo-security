About MFUNGO SECURITY

BRELA registered private security company serving Mwanza and Northwest Tanzania since 2022

Our Company Profile

MFUNGO SECURITY SERVICE COMPANY LIMITED was incorporated on June 6, 2022, as a Private Company Limited by Shares with BRELA Registration Number 156338311. Founded to address the growing security needs in Tanzania's Northwest region, we have established ourselves as a trusted provider of comprehensive security solutions.

Our company is authorized to engage in private security activities (Code 8010), security systems (Code 8020), and compulsory social security activities (Code 8430), serving clients across mining, logistics, retail, and residential sectors around Lake Victoria.

With an authorized share capital of 105,000,000 TZS and a team of experienced professionals, we are committed to enhancing safety and security for businesses and communities in Mwanza and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide professional, reliable, and comprehensive security solutions that protect assets, ensure safety, and enhance business continuity for our clients across Mwanza and Northwest Tanzania.

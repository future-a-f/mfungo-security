About MFUNGO SECURITY

MFUNGO SECURITY SERVICE COMPANY LIMITED is a BRELA-registered private security company (Incorporation No. 156338311) dedicated to enhancing safety in Tanzania's Northwest region since June 2022.

We specialize in private security activities (Code 8010), security systems (Code 8020), and social security activities (Code 8430), serving clients in mining, logistics, retail, and residential sectors.
